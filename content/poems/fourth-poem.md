summary: "Half-sight, half-jokes: Monday, mischief, and waking in water."
tags: ["poetry", "surreal", "family"]
---

A Lazy Man with a Lazy face ,
Wandered lonely in the woods
With only one eye open everytime ,
As he thought the other was of no good.

One day during his evening walk ,
He saw a fox which seemed to talk
But when got closer ,
He realised it was his mother .

Today was Monday and his school,
 He thought of making her mother fool
Would be something very cool,
He dreamed as her mother screamed and found himself in a swimming pool.
