author: "Not Alan Turing"
date: "2026-04-24"
description: "From shine and tears to her mother’s wedding plan—and a quiet meal while life rearranges."
summary: "Love that tried; a mother’s veto; the card; the wedding; carrying on."
tags: ["poetry", "love", "loss"]

Everytime I saw her
I saw her fine
My fantasies came true
Because she was mine

Everytime she cried
I made many efforts I tried and tried and tried 
But she only loved beef
It was her weird belief

Everytime she was happy 
I showed her my score of flappy 
We were never against eachother 
The person who was against was her mother 

She arranged her marriage 
I also got the card
The day of her marriage came
But I continued eating my soup of lard.
